Coverage Selection

Coverage descriptions on this form are general and not comprehensive; final coverage, exclusions, and eligibility depend on the organization’s operations and the terms of the issued policy, so please discuss your specific needs with an insurance consultant who specializes in providing insurance solutions for Nonprofit and Social Service organizations.

Protects the organization, its board members, and leadership against claims alleging wrongful acts in governance or management.

Covers third-party bodily injury and property damage related to your operations or activities.

Covers claims related to professional services such as counseling, casework, program instruction, or other service errors.

Provides wage replacement and medical benefits to employees injured while performing job duties.

Protects against allegations of physical or sexual misconduct by employees, volunteers, or representatives.

Covers owned, leased, rented, or borrowed vehicles — including employee/volunteer vehicles used for work.

Protects buildings, equipment, contents, and other physical assets from fire, theft, vandalism, and more.

Covers lost income and added expenses if operations stop due to a covered property loss.

Protects against theft, fraud, embezzlement, and dishonest acts by employees, volunteers, or third parties.

Provides medical benefits for volunteers accidentally injured during covered nonprofit activities.

Provides protection for short-term fundraisers, events, or other one-time activities.

Covers losses from data breaches, cyberattacks, ransomware, or compromised personal information.

Provides additional liability limits above primary coverages such as GL or Auto.
